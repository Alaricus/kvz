Kitteh vs. Zombie Halloween Game

Kitteh: Lives: Zombie:

Rules:

Kitteh. Has 3 lives. Must smash pumpkins. Gets a new life every 60 pumpkins. Use arrow keys to move.
Zombie. Eats pumpkin brains for a small speed boost. Kills kitteh. Avoid.
Pumpkin. An object of desire.
Candy. Appears when kitteh gets 10 pumpkins. Counts as a pumpkin. Eaten by kitteh, resets zombie's speed. Eaten by zombie, grants a major speed boost.

Tip:

 If kitteh smashes the 11th pumpkin once the candy appears, it will then disapear. Use this knowledge wisely.
